Understanding the Atonement of Jesus Christ

The Atonement is one of the most important doctrine of the gospel of Jesus Christ. Through the Atonement that we can be cleansed, healed, and forgiven. Throught the atonement we can find new strength, new life, and new purpose in Christ.

Our Prophets have said

“However late you think you are, however many chances you think you have missed, however many mistakes you feel you have made … I testify that you have not traveled beyond the reach of divine love.” — Elder Jeffrey R. Holland (The Laborers in the Vineyard, April 2012)

Scriptures about the Atonement

Isaiah 53:5 — "He was wounded for our transgressions, he was bruised for our iniquities..."

Explanation

Alma 7:11–12 — "And he shall go forth, suffering pains and afflictions and temptations of every kind..."

Explanation

Roman 8:38-39 — "For I am persuaded, that neither death, nor life, nor angels, nor principalities, nor powers, nor things present, nor things to come, Nor height, nor depth, nor any other creature, shall be able to separate us from the love of God, which is in Christ Jesus our Lord."

Nothing can separate us from Gods love. Nothing can keep us from the forgiveness that he offers to us. If we choose to follow him he will always forgive us and give us another chance. I love this scripture because it shows how powerful the love of God is and it helps me to feel the power of his atonement, and its ability to bring me to God despite my failings and shortcomings

Personal Experience

In my own life, I have felt the power of the Atonement during times of deep struggle and repentance. The peace that comes from knowing I can be forgiven and strengthened is something I will always treasure.
