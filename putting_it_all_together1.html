Understanding the Atonement of Jesus Christ

The Atonement is the most central and sacred doctrine of the gospel of Jesus Christ. It is through the Atonement that we can be cleansed, healed, and strengthened.

Quotes from Prophets

"The Atonement of Jesus Christ is the supreme expression of our Heavenly Father's love for us." — President Russell M. Nelson

"Through the Atonement of Jesus Christ, all mankind may be saved, by obedience to the laws and ordinances of the Gospel." — Article of Faith #3

Scriptures about the Atonement

Isaiah 53:5 — "He was wounded for our transgressions, he was bruised for our iniquities..."

Alma 7:11–12 — "And he shall go forth, suffering pains and afflictions and temptations of every kind..."

John 3:16 — "For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son..."

My Personal Experience

In my own life, I have felt the power of the Atonement during times of deep struggle and repentance. The peace that comes from knowing I can be forgiven and strengthened is something I will always treasure.
